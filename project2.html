Edward Snowden: Detailed Timeline

This page chronicles the major events, including document copying and leak incidents.

Timeline of Edward Snowden Events
Date Event
April 2012 Begins downloading NSA documents while at Dell contractor role.
December 2012 Contacts journalists anonymously with document samples.
January-March 2013 Joins Booz Allen Hamilton in Hawaii for more document access; continues copying.
May 20, 2013 Arrives in Hong Kong from Hawaii with copied documents to meet journalists.
June 2, 2013 Journalists Glenn Greenwald and Laura Poitras arrive in Hong Kong; receives full documents.
June 5, 2013 The Guardian publishes first story on NSA collecting Verizon phone records.
June 6-7, 2013 Revelations on PRISM program accessing data from Google, Facebook, etc.
June 9, 2013 Snowden publicly identified as source in Guardian interview from Hong Kong.
June 11, 2013 Fired by Booz Allen Hamilton for violating ethics code.
June 22, 2013 U.S. charges Snowden with espionage and theft; requests extradition.
June 23, 2013 Snowden flies from Hong Kong to Moscow on Aeroflot.
July 1, 2013 Applies for asylum in Russia; Putin conditions it on stopping leaks.
August 1, 2013 Granted temporary asylum in Russia after a month in Moscow airport.
2013-Present Lives in Russia; asylum extended, citizenship granted in 2022.
