// Chronological Event Log — Declassified

Detailed Timeline

A chronicle of document copying, leaks, and flight from justice.

April 2012

Begins downloading NSA documents while at Dell contractor role.

December 2012

Contacts journalists anonymously with document samples.

Jan – Mar 2013

Joins Booz Allen Hamilton in Hawaii for more document access; continues copying classified files.

May 20, 2013

Arrives in Hong Kong from Hawaii with copied documents to meet journalists.

June 2, 2013

Journalists Glenn Greenwald and Laura Poitras arrive in Hong Kong; receive the full document cache.

June 5, 2013

The Guardian publishes first story on NSA collecting Verizon phone records in bulk.

June 6–7, 2013

Revelations on PRISM program: NSA accessing data from Google, Facebook, and others.

June 9, 2013

Snowden publicly identified as source in Guardian interview filmed in Hong Kong.

June 11, 2013

Fired by Booz Allen Hamilton for violating the company's ethics code.

June 22, 2013

U.S. charges Snowden with espionage and theft of government property; requests extradition.

June 23, 2013

Snowden flies from Hong Kong to Moscow on Aeroflot; U.S. passport revoked mid-flight.

July 1, 2013

Applies for asylum in Russia; Putin conditions it on stopping intelligence leaks.

August 1, 2013

Granted temporary asylum in Russia after spending over a month in Moscow's Sheremetyevo airport.

2013 – Present

Lives in Russia; asylum repeatedly extended. Russian citizenship granted in 2022.
